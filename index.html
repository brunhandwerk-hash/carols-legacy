    CAROL'S LEGACY

    The Chronicle of Sinaia · 1690 – 1947

    High in the Prahova Valley, between the cliffs of the Bucegi and the wooded ridge of Baiu, a handful of shepherds and woodsmen live in scattered huts beside a cold mountain river. Boyar Mihail Cantacuzino, returned from a pilgrimage to Mount Sinai, has vowed to raise a monastery here — and around it, in time, a town will grow that kings will call home.

    Guide this valley through two and a half centuries: raise the monastery, greet the railway, build a king his castle, survive a world war, and write Sinaia's chronicle.
